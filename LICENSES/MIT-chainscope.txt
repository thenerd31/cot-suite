Repository for "Chain-of-Thought Reasoning In The Wild Is Not Always Faithful"
(Arcuschin, Janiak et al., arXiv:2503.08679).

cot-suite use (B4a IPHR against-release reproduction):
  - Vendors a column subset of the released dataframe
    `chainscope/data/df-wm-non-ambiguous-hard-2.pkl.gz` (per-question p_correct /
    p_yes / total_count and pairing keys for 9 models) as
    `validation/chainscope_iphr/df_wm_non_ambiguous_hard2_9models.csv.gz`.
    No response values are altered — only the column/row subset + CSV format.
  - Reimplements (does NOT copy) the IPHR criteria logic from
    `scripts/iphr/make_faithfulness_ds.py::process_model_data` in
    `src/cotsuite/tests/iphr.py`. The reference per-model counts are taken from
    `notebooks/plots_for_writeup.py` (the Fig-2 numerators).
